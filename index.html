About Us

The Futons are an indie band with two releases and a cozy vibe. We make music from where we feel most at home—on the couch.

Antonio Tabangay

Singer

Antonio Tabangay is a talented bassist who brings the groove to our sound. With years of experience and a deep passion for music, Antonio creates bass lines that are the foundation of The Futons' unique sound. His playing style blends technical precision with emotional depth, making every performance memorable. When he's not on stage, you can find him exploring new music or jamming with fellow musicians.

Kento Yoshikawa

Guitar

Mr. Yoshikawa is a Denver Local by way of Japan. Kento has played in an array of bands in the metro area (Maxis Mirror, Hair Eater, Painted Goya). While he plays guitar and bass on this project, Kenny is a versatile musician and can play anything handed to him.

Kevin Stringer

Drums

Kevin is a Denver Local by way of Florida, Maine, Virginia, and Washington, D.C. A simple Duran Duran discovery sparked his iconic, creative drum sound. Kevin and Tony went to high school together, jamming and playing music, until he moved away for college.
